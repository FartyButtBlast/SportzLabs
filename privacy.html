SportzLabs Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Overview

SportzLabs builds sports intelligence products and related websites. This policy explains the information used by the SportzLabs corporate site and linked SportzLabs apps.

Corporate Website

The SportzLabs website is a marketing and information site. If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the information you choose to include in your message so we can respond.

Linked Apps

SportzLabs apps may use account login, analytics, performance data, and links to third-party publishers. Each app may have more specific privacy details. For AFL Performance, account registration, email verification, login, and password reset are handled through Supabase.

Analytics

SportzLabs apps may use analytics tools, including Google Analytics, to understand product usage and improve the experience. Analytics events are intended to measure app usage and are not intended to collect passwords or sensitive account credentials.

Third-Party Services

SportzLabs products may link to external websites, data providers, news publishers, authentication services, analytics providers, or app stores. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Account And Support Requests

To request support, ask a privacy question, or request account deletion for a SportzLabs app, contact support@sportzlabs.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated as SportzLabs products change. The updated date on this page will reflect the latest version.
