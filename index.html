FAQ

Simple answers about Spotzlabs.

What is Spotzlabs?

Spotzlabs builds simple intelligence apps for sport, performance and training.

What is the AFL analytics app for?

The AFL analytics app helps fantasy football, SuperCoach and gaming users compare players, track performance trends, view team dashboards and make more informed decisions.

Who is the AFL app built for?

It is built for AFL fans, fantasy football users, SuperCoach players, gaming users and people who want clearer AFL performance insights.

Will Spotzlabs only focus on AFL?

No. AFL analytics is the first app. Spotzlabs will also add other performance and training tools, including a Workout Studio app.
